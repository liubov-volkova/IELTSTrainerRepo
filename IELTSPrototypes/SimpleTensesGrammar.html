Present simple

Basic grammar rules

Indicators and usage

Indicator words
Usage
every day often always usually sometimes never seldom first ... then .., after that
  1. repeated actions (every day, always, often, sometimes, never):
    • We have English lessons every day.
    • They often go for a walk.
    • They always drink coffee in the morning.
    • I usually run with my friends.
    • He sometimes looks tierd.
    • She never feels alone.
    • It seldom brings happiness.
  2. Things in general:
    • The sun rises in the east.
    • Cats drink milk.
  3. Fixed arrangements, scheduled events (e.g. timetable):
    • The plane flies to London every Monday.
    • We have English on Mondays and Fridays.
  4. Sequence of actions in the present (first â€“ then, after that):
    • First your books at page 34, then read it. After that retell the whole story.
    • Sit down and relax!
  5. Instructions:
    • Never open this door!
    • Don't forget your umbrella!

  • I lived in London last winter
  • His parents married in 2010.
  • They drank coffee in the restorant yeaterday.
  • I met him two mpnths ago.
